Pool Features
Equihash Algorithm
MineTheChains is a multi-coin pool for mining Equihash algorithm based Cryptocurrencies. This means you can mine multiple chains, all on the one pool!
Use NiceHash & MRR
You can mine using Nicehash & MiningRigRentals! Just use the appropriate port listed on the getting started page.
Anonymous Mining
There is no sign up or registration required. Just connect with your address and mine! We also advise using a specific mining address, one not related to your normal wallet, to maximise your anonymity whilst mining.
Australian Servers
MineTheChains provides miners with an Australian server to connect to. Improving connection times and maximising share input.
Small Fee
A small fee covers the cost of the server hosting and the hosts time. It is automatically taken out of the payments and is verifiable on the block explorer!
Easy To Setup
Check out the Getting Started page for simple instructions on how to set your miner up and get mining!
